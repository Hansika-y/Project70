Campus Life

Walters Middle School

Was established in 1997

This school has been running for 25 years and is very organized. There are many clubs, sporting events, and parties. This school pays attention to the students needs and always gives the best it can give.



school library

Was built in 1999

The library has wonderful books that range range from comedy to mystery. Also, it has books for all the ages in school; 11-14. Shuba Barathidasan donated a huge sum to this place and still continues to do so.



school auditorium

Was built in 1998

The auditorium can hold over a thousand ppl at a time. It is a great place to hold events and parties. This place is also where awarding ceremonies, anouncements, assemblies, and performances are held.
